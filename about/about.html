COMPUTER MUSEUM
COMPANY

The Company brings together the community of experts, academics, and activists who engage the Company affiliate network in a different country every two years.
This year, we hope to expand our invitation list incluiding organizations and individuals who want to work with us om shared proyects that advance the cause of the Commons, free culture and open knowledge. So if you are active and engaged in the words of open content and knowledge - free software advocates, Wikipedians, Open knowledge, galleries, libraries, museums, archives, governmments and foundations, lawyers, and activists - we hope you ill consider joining us this year to build a stronger, move vibrant commons together.
